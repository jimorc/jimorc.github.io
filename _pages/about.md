## Website

This website is my personal blog covering the following topics:
- Photography
- Computing in General
- Computer Programming
- Information Security. There are many sites that cover information security for 
commercial enterprises. There is much less information covering information security
for individuals. Some of the posts on this site will cover this topic.
- Other Tech Topics as I See Fit to Cover

## Me

I have more than five decades of experience in photography and computing, including
programming.

### Computer Programming

My first computer was an IBM 360/75 at University where I learned Fortran programming starting in 1968. Following University, I spent many years maintaining
and then creating computer programs and systems in a business environment. Since I
retired, I have maintained an interest in programming and computer software.

### Information Security

The last several years of my working life was spent in Information Security. As I
noted above, I intend to cover Information Security topics for individuals rather
than commerical enterprises and businesses.

### Photography

I purchased my first *serious* camera[^1] in 1972. Since then, I have owned and
used several more 35 mm and digital cameras. I am not now, nor have I ever been
a professional photographer. I also do not consider myself a photography expert; I
am an enthusiastic amateur who feels he can help others on their path to enjoying
the hobby. 

[^1]: A Pentax Spotmatic 1000 SLR.
